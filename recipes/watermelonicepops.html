Watermelon Ice Pops

Description

Cool and refreshing, with no artificial colors or flavors! If you don't have ice pop molds, you can use paper cups and wooden sticks.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups watermelon, seeded and diced
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Blend watermelon, water, honey, lemon juice, and sugar together in a blender until smooth. Pour mixture into ice pop molds and freeze until solid, about 2 hours.
  2. Run hot water over ice pop molds for a few seconds to unmold.
